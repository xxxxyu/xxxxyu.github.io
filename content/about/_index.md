I am a 4th year Ph.D. candidate at [Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR)](https://air.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), Tsinghua University, advised by Dr. [Yunxin Liu](https://yunxinliu.github.io/). Prior to this, I received my B.E. degree from Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in 2022.

My research is focused on **on-device and physical AI**. Main topics:

- **Efficient inference system** (main line during Ph.D.):
  - [FlexNN](https://dl.acm.org/doi/pdf/10.1145/3636534.3649391) (MobiCom 2024): efficient and adaptive memory management.
  - [Vec-LUT](https://www.arxiv.org/abs/2512.06443) (MobiSys 2026): GeMM optimization for ultra-low-bit LLMs.
  - KV cache management for VLA: coming soon.
- **Autonomous embodied agent** (recent interest):
  - Pre-training and model architecture.
  - Post-training self-evolvement.
